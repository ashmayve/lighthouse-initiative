What We Do

Big dreams.
Real actions.

We show up, supply up, and teach up — giving orphaned children the tools and skills to imagine a bigger future for themselves.

🎨
Creative Art Kits
We deliver hand-curated art kits filled with pencils, pens, paints, paper, and notebooks. Because creativity is the first language of confidence.

How It Works

Simple process.
Lasting impact.

From signing up to sitting beside a child with a pencil in hand — here's how Lighthouse works.

01
✍️
Sign Up
Fill out a short form. All backgrounds welcome — if you care about kids and learning, you belong here.
02
📋
Orientation
A quick 30-min online session covering guidelines, safety policy, and how to connect meaningfully with the children.
03
🎒
Visit & Teach
Join our team at the orphanage. Teach, create art, and build real connections that last beyond the session.
04
🏅
Get Certified
Receive an official Lighthouse volunteering certificate. Your time and care are recognized and valued.
