What We Do

Big dreams.
Real actions.

We connect, supply up, and teach up — giving orphaned children the tools and skills to imagine a bigger future for themselves.

🎨
Creative Art Kits
We deliver hand-curated art kits filled with pencils, pens, paints, paper, and notebooks. Because creativity is the first language of confidence.
Get involved →
📐
Math & English Tutoring
University volunteers lead structured online tutoring sessions with children at partner orphanages, making sure every child gets real, personalized academic support.

🤝
Mentorship & Connection
Beyond lessons — our volunteers become role models. Consistent online check-ins build the kind of trust and connection that transforms a child's self-belief.

How It Works

Simple process.
Lasting impact.

From signing up to connecting with a child online — here's how Lighthouse works.

01
✍️
Sign Up
Fill out a short form. All backgrounds welcome — if you care about kids and learning, you belong here.
02
📋
Orientation
A quick 30-min online session covering guidelines, safety policy, and how to connect meaningfully with the children.
03
🎒
Connect & Teach
Join our team online. Tutor, create art together, and build real connections that last beyond the session.
04
🏅
Get Certified
Receive an official Lighthouse volunteering certificate. Your time and care are recognized and valued.
